STOPPING CLOTHES MOTHS

Clothes moths tend to stay confined near clothing, which makes them easier to control. To protect your belongings, do not store boxes of clothes in the attic, garage or basement, if possible. In closets, store natural fiber clothing items by covering them with individual plastic covers.

And, of course, if you think clothes moths are in you’re home, call ®. We have the solutions to get them out.

HOW WE STOP COCKROACHES

® professionals begin by inspecting and treating available hiding places in which cockroaches might live.

Vacuuming may be used in some cases to provide immediate reduction in the number of cockroaches. A variety of application techniques and methods may then be used to provide a comprehensive cockroach treatment, including crack and crevice treatments, void treatments and baits.

EFFECTIVE HOUSE ANT CONTROL

House ants can be difficult to control because they establish multiple subcolonies and may nest in a wide variety of sites. The key to control is finding and treating the colonies and subcolonies. That’s where ® comes in.

GETTING RID OF MICE

Mice are incredibly elusive and can cause serious property damage, electrical fires included. And they only need 3 to 4 grams of food per day to survive. By far, the easiest and best way to get rid of mice is to call the professionals at ®.

GETTING RID OF RATS

Rats can cause serious damage to your home. Common indicators of a rat problem include finding droppings or evidence of gnawing, seeing rat tracks, hearing scurrying sounds or squeaking in walls, smelling a musky scent, finding burrows or even seeing dead rats.

® technicians can get rats terrorizing your home out. Call today for your free pest estimate.

STOPPING SCORPIONS

Distinguishing the differences between various species of scorpions can be a challenge. However, knowing how to identify them and where they are commonly located can be helpful information. A ® technician can help you identify the species in your home and determine a way to get them out.

EFFECTIVE SILVERFISH CONTROL

If you think silverfish are in your home, removal may require the application of insecticides. For safety, this is a job best left to a professional. A ® technician can develop a strategy for dealing with the silverfish and preventing future outbreaks.

HOW TO GET RID OF SPIDERS

Some spiders may be living outdoors in piles of firewood and debris, under items lying on the ground or in voids in hollow blocks, while others may live in attics and crawl spaces or other areas throughout the home. A ® technician will reveal these hiding spots and help eliminate spiders from your home.
