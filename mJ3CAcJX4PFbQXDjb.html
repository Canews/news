Forex volumes: online, where to watch in real time, what it is

Profitable trading depends not only on the acquired knowledge and market research, but also on the factors that affect its operation. Of great importance is not only the regular analysis of the state of the exchange, but also the volume of trading on the exchange Forex . Even when trading according to all the rules and strategies, many investors lose their money. This happens when large market makers appear on the Forex market, setting their terms on the exchange.

What is volume in the forex market

Market makers trade Forex only on a large scale, thus significantly affecting exchange pricing. Some novice investors do not know what volume is and why it is needed .

This is the number of exchange operations that are performed over a set period of time.

In the implementation of trade transactions should be you must analyze the VSA. This takes into account not only the number of transactions, but also the location of candlesticks. Market analysts regularly monitor Forex volumes, making various strategic forecasts.

You can use VSA to find out:

  • In which direction traders are trading. I.e. the process moves towards the concentration of the largest number of assets.
  • The time when you should not enter into a trade.
  • A period when the trend movement cannot be predicted.

Analyzing the market through VSA, special volume indicators are created that provide information about investors who work with their brokers.

The most reliable Forex volumes online can be found at such major brokers as Alpari, which have the maximum number of clients. But even they can't say what the actual volume of transactions carried out on the exchange is.

Kinds volumes'

Volumes on Forex are divided into several different types:

  • Quantitative . This is the total number of transactions that are made during the specified time period. It allows any investor to analyze the distribution of the number of transactions made inside the candle at different prices. as support and resistance, indicate the points where traders trade most actively.
  • Exchange-traded Fund . This is the number of shares that have been bought or sold by traders.
  • Teak . It depends on the speed of price changes, differs from the quantitative one, but reflects it fairly well interests of all participants of the exchange.

The volume of stock trading

Volume on the stock exchange is the number of transactions with shares that are traded on the stock exchange. they were committed during the specified period of time. The investor is guided by 3 such parameters as price, time and volume. The last indicator on the stock market corresponds to the number of shares that were traded when they were purchased/ sold. If a trader buys 40 shares, the volume will be 40. If he sells 20 of them, the total volume will be already 60.

How does the market volume differ from teak

With the right approach, indicators will help you see how the exchange's participants behave and comprehensively analyze the situation on it.

And not to give away your money for market makers, it is necessary to regularly monitor their manipulations, guess their future steps and open transactions in the same direction as them.

Description of signals of the better volume indicator

The improved Better Volume indicator can independently evaluate the existing volume and spread of the candle, compare them with previous indicators, and issue signals that will tell you about the number of assets and the level of demand.

What do the colors of the signals on the chart indicate?:

  • Red corresponds to a large number of assets and a wide spread on the up bar. Occurs at the start and finish of the trend climbs as well as during the period of correction on the descending trend.
  • White color corresponds to a large number of assets and a wide spread on the down bar. Occurs at the start and finish of declining trends, as well as during corrections on rising trends.
  • The yellow color corresponds to a small number of shares. Occurs at the end of trends and during corrections.
  • The green color corresponds to the presence of a bar with a minimum spread and a large volume. Occurs at the end of trends, when stocks they are passed from market makers to traders, and when profits are made in the middle of trading.
